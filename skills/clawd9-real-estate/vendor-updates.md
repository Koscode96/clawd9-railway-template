# Vendor Updates Skill

## Purpose
Keep property vendors (sellers) informed with regular updates, viewing feedback, and market insights to maintain engagement and trust throughout the sales process.

## Capabilities
- Send automated weekly updates
- Communicate viewing feedback
- Share market intelligence
- Handle price reduction discussions
- Manage vendor expectations

## Compliance Notes

- Never guarantee sale prices or timescales
- Always disclose if a buyer is connected to the agency
- Keep records of all price discussions
- Escalate any complaints to branch manager
- Do not share one vendor's information with another
